About Me

Hey, I am Jesse Zonneveld, a hardworking and enthusiastic web developer. I discovered my passion for web development back when I was a kid in my very first computer science class. I remember playing around with my first HTML tag and setting its color to change when my mouse hovered over it. Back then I never thought it was possible to turn my passion of seeing my ideas become tangible features on the web to making fully functional websites and web applications.

Today, almost 15 years later, after many life events (travelling the world, graduating with a Bachelor of Engineering, getting married to my wonderful wife) I have rediscovered my passion for web development. I have put my engineering problem solving skills and work ethic to good use by studying web development for the better half of the last year. I have learned how to make custom WordPress websites and JavaScript applications, as well as understand and know how to use GitHub, SCSS, React, NPM and Webpack.

I am excited to see where this passion and new career takes me. In the meantime, I will continue to learn and experiment with the latest web development software and techniques. Please feel free to reach out and connect with me, it would be great to share my web development journey and current projects with you.
